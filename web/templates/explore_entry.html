Explore Structure Detail Page

The PDBrt database contains individual protein-ligand entries that provide detailed information about the binding kinetics as well as cross-links to many external resources for macromolecular structure data.


Download Option

At the top of the page you can download zipped complex data in pdb (for complex and protein)/sdf (for ligand) format.




3D View

Each Structure Detail page has specific sections that present different types of information about the structure. On the top left, under the Structure PDB ID, snapshot box provides a quick glimpse of what the contents of the entry look like. Each protein chain instance is assigned a unique color and presented in the cartoon representation by default. Ligands are shown in ball-and-stick representations.

Click on the hyperlinked words below the snapshots to view the structure interactively in 3Dmol in different representation: line, stick, cross, cartoon or surface. To remove surface representation click on the "remove surface" button.
